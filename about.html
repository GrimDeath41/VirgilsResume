I hope you will take me into consideration for your property.
I promise I will do my best in taking care of it!

I am a quiet and gentle old boy and will spend most of the day sleeping on the couch (or on the bed if I’m not caught!).

I can only really go on a 30 minute a day walk before tiring and needing to spend the rest of the day sleeping.

I would be described as a low maintenance dog

Unlike most Dachshunds I'm not one to bark or growl (I would make a terrible guard dog), needing very little space.

I'm happy and not at all destructive.
If I’m not sleeping, I’m chasing down my owner for pats.
